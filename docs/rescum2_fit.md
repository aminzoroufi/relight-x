# RESCu-M2 Fit

ReLight-X is not only a smart lighting project. It is a circular smart infrastructure demonstrator.

## Circular Infrastructure Value

- Retrofitting existing LED roadway luminaires instead of replacing full assets.
- Reducing energy consumption through adaptive dimming.
- Reducing thermal and electrical stress by avoiding unnecessary 100% operation.
- Monitoring luminaire and driver health.
- Estimating remaining useful life.
- Creating digital product passports.
- Supporting reuse, repair, remanufacture, and recycle decisions.
- Using a digital twin to validate control before field work.
- Using sensing, vision classification, automation, and edge control for lifecycle intelligence.

## Research Contribution

The demonstrator connects:

- Adaptive highway lighting.
- Emergency-aware safety control.
- Cost-effective mmWave sensing.
- Optional computer vision classification.
- Digital twin validation.
- Edge controller board feasibility.
- Energy and carbon accounting.
- Product passport and Re-X lifecycle decisions.

## Interview/Proposal Framing

ReLight-X can be presented as a practical prototype pathway:

1. Simulate highway traffic and lighting behavior.
2. Validate controls in Unity.
3. Test board-level PWM/HIL output in the lab.
4. Collect simulated health and energy data.
5. Use digital passports to demonstrate circular asset decisions.
6. Define a realistic path toward DALI/D4i and certified roadway integration.
