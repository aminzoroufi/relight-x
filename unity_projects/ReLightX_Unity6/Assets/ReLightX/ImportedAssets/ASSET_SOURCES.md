# Imported Visualization Assets

These assets are used only for the Unity digital twin visualization.

## Kenney Car Kit

- Source: https://kenney.nl/assets/car-kit
- License: Creative Commons CC0
- Imported models: normal cars, SUV, taxi, van, police car, ambulance, firetruck, truck.

## Poly Haven Street Lamp 01

- Source: https://polyhaven.com/a/street_lamp_01
- License: CC0
- Imported files: 1K FBX and lightweight textures.

The generated highway poles are still procedural because the ReLight-X scene needs dual highway luminaires per pole. The downloaded street-lamp asset is included as a realistic lighting reference/environment asset.
